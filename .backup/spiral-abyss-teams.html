Team Building for Abyss

You need TWO teams of 4 characters. They can't share characters, so you need two distinct teams with different DPS carries. Ideally, each team has: DPS + Sub-DPS + Support + Sustain.

Proven Abyss Teams

Team 1: Rational (Raiden National)

Raiden + Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett. Reliable Vaporize damage, easy rotation.

Team 2: Hyperbloom

Nahida + Xingqiu + Kuki Shinobu + flex. Low investment, high damage floor.

Alternative: Freeze

Ayaka + Kokomi + Kazuha + Shenhe. Great when enemies can be frozen.

General Tips

  • Check the current blessing — build teams that benefit from it
  • Floor 12 changes every version — check which elements are favored
  • Energy Recharge matters more in Abyss than in overworld
  • If you can't 36-star, aim for 33 — the last 50 primos aren't worth the stress
