TL;DR

Follow the Archon Quest. Spend resin on leylines and bosses (not artifacts before AR 45). Save all Primogems for limited banners — never spend on standard. Focus on 4-6 characters max. The free characters (Kaeya, Xiangling, Barbara) can clear all story content.

FAQ

Should I pull on the current banner as a new player?

Only if the featured 5-star is a top-tier support or DPS. Check our character guides for tier rankings. Never pull on weapon banner as F2P. Your first limited 5-star should be versatile — Kazuha, Nahida, Furina, or Zhongli are the best first pulls.

How fast can I reach endgame?

About 2-3 months to reach AR 45-50 with daily play. Story content provides enough AR EXP. Don't rush — there's no competitive endgame timer. The journey is the content.
