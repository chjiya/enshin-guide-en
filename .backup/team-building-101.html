TL;DR

Every team needs 4 roles: Main DPS, Sub-DPS, Support, Sustain. The best teams leverage elemental reactions: Hyperbloom (cheapest to build), Vaporize (highest ceiling), or Aggravate (balanced). The National team (Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett + flex) has been a top-5 Abyss team for 4+ years — all 4-stars.

FAQ

What's the best team for a new player?

Hyperbloom. It only needs Elemental Mastery on one character (Kuki Shinobu or Raiden). The damage floor is extremely high. A Hyperbloom team with level 80 characters and 3★ weapons can clear Abyss Floor 12.
