What We Know

Natlan is the Pyro nation, ruled by the Pyro Archon. The region features unique traversal mechanics tied to Saurians — dragon-like creatures that help with exploration.

What to Prepare

  • Save Primogems — new Archons are always meta-defining
  • Farm Pyro ascension gems
  • Complete previous Archon quests (required to enter Natlan)
