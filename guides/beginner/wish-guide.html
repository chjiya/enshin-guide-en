Pity System Explained

  • Hard pity: 90 wishes guarantees a 5-star
  • Soft pity: Starting at wish 74, 5-star rate increases dramatically
  • 50/50: Your first 5-star has 50% chance to be the featured character. If you lose, the next one is guaranteed.

How Many Wishes to Save

To guarantee a limited 5-star through worst-case scenario (lose 50/50, go to 90 hard pity twice): 28,800 Primogems = 180 wishes. Realistically, 150-160 wishes is enough thanks to soft pity.

Should You Pull on Weapon Banner?

Generally no for F2P. The weapon banner requires up to 240 wishes to guarantee a specific weapon (Epitomized Path). Characters > weapons, always.
