The 4 Roles Every Team Needs

  • Main DPS: Your damage dealer. Spends the most time on-field. Example: Hu Tao, Neuvillette.
  • Sub-DPS: Deals damage off-field. Example: Xiangling, Fischl, Xingqiu.
  • Support/Buffer: Boosts team damage. Example: Bennett, Kazuha, Furina.
  • Sustain: Healer or shielder. Example: Zhongli, Kokomi, Jean.

Elemental Synergy

The best teams leverage elemental reactions:

  • Vaporize: Hydro + Pyro = 1.5x or 2x damage
  • Melt: Pyro + Cryo = 1.5x or 2x damage
  • Hyperbloom: Dendro + Hydro + Electro = tracking missiles
  • Aggravate: Dendro + Electro = flat damage bonus
