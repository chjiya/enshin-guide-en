Artifact Guide

[Ad]

TL;DR

Don't farm artifacts before AR 45. After AR 45, focus on universal sets: Emblem of Severed Fate, Deepwood Memories, Golden Troupe, Marechaussee Hunter. A correct main stat with bad substats beats a wrong main stat with perfect substats.

Community Standards for "Good Enough"

  • DPS: 60/120 CRIT ratio, correct main stats on Sands/Goblet/Circlet
  • Support: Correct main stats, enough ER to burst every rotation
  • Hyperbloom trigger: 900+ EM, level 90 (transformative reactions scale with level)

FAQ

How long to farm a "good" artifact set?

2-4 weeks of daily resin for a usable set, 2-3 months for an optimized one. Community data from Genshin Optimizer shows the average resin cost for a 60/120 CRIT build is ~8,000 resin (50 days). Accept "good enough" and move on.
