How Artifacts Work

Each character has 5 artifact slots: Flower (HP), Plume (ATK), Sands, Goblet, and Circlet. 2-piece and 4-piece set bonuses activate when equipping matching artifacts from the same set.

Main Stats by Slot

  • Sands: ATK%, DEF%, HP%, Elemental Mastery, or Energy Recharge%
  • Goblet: Elemental DMG% or Physical DMG% (rarest correct main stat!)
  • Circlet: CRIT Rate%, CRIT DMG%, or Healing Bonus%

When to Farm

AR 45+ only. Before AR 45, use whatever artifacts you find from bosses and chests. At AR 45, the highest-level artifact domains unlock and guarantee a 5-star artifact per run.

Substat Priority

For DPS: CRIT Rate ≈ CRIT DMG > ATK% > EM (for reactions) > ER%. Each artifact at +4, +8, +12, +16, +20 randomly upgrades one substat. "God rolls" are artifacts where all upgrades hit CRIT.
