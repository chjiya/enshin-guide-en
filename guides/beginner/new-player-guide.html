New Player Guide — Your First Month in Teyvat

Day 1 Priorities

  • Follow the main story quest — it unlocks everything
  • Unlock all Statues of the Seven — they reveal the map and give stamina
  • Spend your Original Resin on Ley Line Blossoms (Mora and EXP books)
  • Don't spend Primogems on the standard banner — save for limited characters

Resin: Your Most Valuable Resource

Original Resin regenerates over time (1 per 8 minutes, capped at 160). Use it daily. Early game priority: Character ascension bosses > Talent books > Weapon materials > Artifacts (AR 45+)

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Spending Primogems on the standard banner
  • Leveling every character — focus on 4-6
  • Farming artifacts before AR 45 (5-star artifacts guaranteed at that point)
  • Using Fragile Resin before AR 45

AR Progression Roadmap

  • AR 1-20: Mondstadt story. Get free characters (Kaeya, Lisa, Amber).
  • AR 20-30: Liyue story. Unlock Xiangling from Spiral Abyss Floor 3.
  • AR 30-40: Inazuma. Start thinking about artifact sets.
  • AR 45+: Endgame. Farm 5-star artifacts. Challenge Spiral Abyss.
