Monthly Primogem Income (F2P)

  • Daily Commissions: 60/day × 30 = 1,800 primos
  • Spiral Abyss (36★): 1,200 primos per cycle × 2 = 2,400/month
  • Events: ~1,000-2,000/month
  • Paimon's Bargains: 5 Intertwined Fate/month (800 primos equivalent)
  • Exploration + Quests: Depends on content, ~500-2,000/month

Total estimate: 6,000-8,000 primos per month, or about 40-50 pulls. Enough for a 5-star every 1.5-2 months.

Maximizing Income

  • Do your dailies — it's 60 primos guaranteed
  • Push Spiral Abyss as far as you can — every star counts
  • Never miss limited events — they're the second biggest source
  • Open every chest you see while exploring
