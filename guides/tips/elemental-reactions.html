Amplifying Reactions (Crit-able)

  • Vaporize: Pyro + Hydro. Pyro trigger = 1.5x, Hydro trigger = 2x damage
  • Melt: Pyro + Cryo. Pyro trigger = 2x, Cryo trigger = 1.5x damage

Transformative Reactions (Dendro Meta)

  • Hyperbloom: Dendro + Hydro (Bloom seed) + Electro → homing Dendro missiles. Scales with EM of Electro character only. Currently the strongest reaction for low investment.
  • Burgeon: Dendro + Hydro (seed) + Pyro → AoE Dendro explosion
  • Aggravate: Dendro + Electro (+ Electro) → flat damage bonus to Electro attacks
  • Spread: Dendro + Electro (+ Dendro) → flat damage bonus to Dendro attacks

Support Reactions

  • Swirl: Anemo + any element (except Geo/Dendro) → spreads element + VV shred
  • Superconduct: Cryo + Electro → AoE Cryo damage + 40% Physical RES shred
  • Freeze: Hydro + Cryo → immobilizes enemies

Which Reaction Is Best?

For new players: Hyperbloom (lowest investment, highest floor). For veterans: Vaporize/Melt (highest ceiling with good artifacts). Aggravate is a strong middle ground.
