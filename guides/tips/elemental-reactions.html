FAQ

What's the strongest reaction in 5.7?

Hyperbloom for low investment, Vaporize for high investment. Hyperbloom's damage floor is the highest — a level 90 Kuki with 900 EM deals 30K+ per seed. Vaporize scales with CRIT and has the highest ceiling but requires good artifacts.
