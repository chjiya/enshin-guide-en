Raiden Shogun Guide — The Eternal Battery (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Raiden Shogun is a battery, sub-DPS, and main DPS. Her burst regenerates energy for the entire team. The Rational team (Raiden + Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett) has been a top-5 Abyss team since 2.1 — the longest streak of any team composition.

Community Consensus

Raiden has remained S-tier since 2.1. The Rational team's longevity comes from Bennett + Xiangling's broken synergy — Raiden just enables it. KQM data shows National variants account for 25%+ of Abyss clears across all patches.

Optimal Build

  • Artifact: 4pc Emblem of Severed Fate
  • Main Stats: ER% / ATK% / Crit (250%+ ER target)
  • Weapon: Engulfing Lightning or The Catch (free, R5 from fishing!)

FAQ

The Catch or Engulfing Lightning?

Engulfing is ~14% better. But R5 The Catch is free and completely sufficient for 36★ Abyss. Only pull Engulfing if you're a Raiden main who wants to optimize.
