Why Raiden Is Still Meta

Raiden Shogun is a battery, sub-DPS, and main DPS rolled into one. Her burst regenerates energy for the entire team, enabling burst-heavy compositions. Her skill provides off-field Electro application with near-100% uptime.

The Rational Team (Raiden National)

  • Raiden Shogun — Driver + battery
  • Xiangling — Pyronado off-field DPS
  • Xingqiu — Hydro application for Vaporize
  • Bennett — Healer + massive ATK buff

This team has been a top Spiral Abyss pick since Raiden's release. Simple rotation, massive damage, all 4-stars except Raiden.

Artifact & Weapon

  • Artifact: 4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate — converts ER to Burst DMG
  • Main Stats: ER% / ATK% or Electro DMG / CRIT
  • Weapon: Engulfing Lightning or The Catch (free from fishing!)
  • Target: 250%+ Energy Recharge
