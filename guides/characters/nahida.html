Nahida Guide — The Dendro Archon (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Nahida is the best Dendro applicator in Genshin. Her skill links enemies together, dealing Dendro damage whenever any linked enemy takes an elemental reaction. She enables Hyperbloom, Burgeon, and Aggravate teams. At C0 with 1000 EM, she provides 250 EM to the active character.

Community Consensus

SS-tier since 3.2. KQM ranks her as the single highest-value pull for account progression due to Dendro's accessibility. Community data shows Hyperbloom teams with Nahida clear Spiral Abyss at the lowest investment level of any archetype.

Optimal Build

  • On-field: 4pc Deepwood, EM/Dendro DMG/Crit
  • Off-field: 4pc Deepwood, EM/EM/EM (1000 EM target)
  • Weapon: A Thousand Floating Dreams or Sacrificial Fragments (F2P)

FAQ

Is C2 Nahida worth it?

C2 is one of the best constellations in the game. It allows Bloom reactions to crit (20% fixed rate, 100% CDMG) and gives Quicken reactions 30% DEF shred. Approximately 18% team damage increase.
