Why Nahida Is Top Tier

Nahida is the best Dendro applicator in the game. Her skill links enemies together, dealing Dendro damage whenever a linked enemy takes an elemental reaction. This enables Hyperbloom, Burgeon, and Aggravate teams to function at maximum efficiency.

Build Options

On-Field Driver

  • Artifact: 4-piece Deepwood Memories
  • Main Stats: EM / Dendro DMG / CRIT
  • Weapon: A Thousand Floating Dreams or Sacrificial Fragments

Off-Field Support

  • Artifact: 4-piece Deepwood Memories
  • Main Stats: EM / EM / EM
  • Goal: 1000 Elemental Mastery

Best Teams

  • Hyperbloom: Nahida + Xingqiu + Kuki Shinobu (full EM) + flex
  • Aggravate: Nahida + Yae Miko + Fischl + Zhongli
  • Nilou Bloom: Nahida + Nilou + Kokomi + flex Dendro
