Kazuha Guide — The Anemo King

[Ad Placeholder]

Why Kazuha Is Irreplaceable

Kazuha provides: enemy grouping, 40% elemental RES shred (via VV set), and an elemental DMG% buff that scales with his Elemental Mastery. He's the premium support for any reaction-based team.

Double Swirl Technique

Kazuha can buff TWO elements simultaneously. Apply Element A to an enemy, apply Element B to yourself (via Bennett's self-Pyro, for example), then use Kazuha's skill. He'll swirl both elements, giving your team +DMG% for both.

Build

  • Artifact: 4-piece Viridescent Venerer
  • Main Stats: EM / EM / EM — stack as much Elemental Mastery as possible
  • Weapon: Freedom-Sworn or Iron Sting (craftable F2P)
  • Goal: 900+ EM

Best Teams

Any team that does elemental damage. International (Childe + Xiangling), Ayaka Freeze, Raiden Hypercarry, Aggravate — Kazuha makes them all better.
