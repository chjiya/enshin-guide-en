Kazuha Guide — The Anemo King (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Kazuha provides enemy grouping, 40% elemental RES shred (via VV), and elemental DMG% buff equal to 0.04% × his Elemental Mastery. At 1000 EM, that's 40% DMG bonus. He can buff two elements simultaneously via Double Swirl technique.

Community Consensus

Kazuha has been SS-tier since 1.6 — the longest unbroken SS streak of any character. KQM data shows he's the most-used character in Abyss across all servers (90%+ usage rate at C0).

FAQ

Kazuha or Xilonen?

Kazuha for reaction teams, Xilonen for mono-element. Xilonen provides longer RES shred but no grouping. Kazuha's grouping + VV makes him better for reaction teams. They stack together in some premium teams (Neuvillette Hypercarry).
