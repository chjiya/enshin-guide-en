Furina Guide — Universal Buffer (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Furina provides up to 75% team-wide DMG% buff at C0. Her Fanfare mechanic converts HP fluctuations into damage — you need a team-wide healer (Jean, Baizhu, Charlotte) to cap Fanfare stacks. She's the highest-value buffer in Genshin alongside Kazuha.

Community Consensus

SS-tier since 4.2. KQM ranks Furina as the #1 priority pull for veteran accounts. She enables a new healer meta — characters like Jean and Noelle went from B-tier to A-tier purely because they synergize with Furina's HP drain mechanic.

Budget Team

Furina + Jean (free from anniversary) + any DPS + flex. Jean's burst instantly caps Fanfare stacks for the full 75% DMG buff.

FAQ

Is C2 Furina worth it?

C2 is her best constellation. It increases Fanfare generation by 250%, letting her cap stacks in ~3 seconds even without a team-wide healer. C2 Furina can run with single-target healers like Bennett.
