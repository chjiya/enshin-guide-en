Furina Guide — The Universal Buffer

[Ad Placeholder]

Why Furina Is the Best Buffer

Furina provides a team-wide DMG% buff that scales up to 75%+ at C0. Her Fanfare mechanic converts HP fluctuations into damage — the more your team's HP changes (healing or draining), the bigger her buff. She also deals significant off-field Hydro damage with her summons.

How Fanfare Works

Furina's burst creates a "Fanfare" meter. Every time a party member's HP changes, Fanfare stacks increase, boosting team-wide DMG%. This means you want a team-wide healer (Jean, Baizhu, Charlotte) to rapidly cap Fanfare stacks.

Best Team Comps

  • Furina + Jean + any DPS: Jean's burst insta-caps Fanfare
  • Neuvillette + Furina: HP drain + heal synergy
  • Hu Tao + Furina + Yelan: Double Hydro, HP manipulation

Artifact & Weapon

  • Artifact: 4-piece Golden Troupe — boosts off-field skill damage
  • Weapon: Splendor of Tranquil Waters or Festering Desire (F2P)
