Zhongli Guide — The Unbreakable Shield (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Zhongli has the strongest shield in Genshin Impact. At 50,000 HP, his Jade Shield absorbs 20,000+ damage and has 100% uptime. It also shreds all enemy elemental RES by 20% — a universal damage buff. He's the most-pulled character in Genshin history per community banner data.

Community Consensus

Zhongli has been SS-tier for shields since 1.3. KQM (KeqingMains) theorycrafting confirms he provides the highest effective HP increase of any character. Reddit polls consistently rank him as the #1 comfort pick for Spiral Abyss.

Optimal Shield Build

  • Artifact: 4pc Tenacity of the Millelith (20% ATK + 30% shield strength for team)
  • Main Stats: HP% / HP% / HP% — all HP, no exceptions
  • Weapon: Black Tassel (3★, 46.9% HP) outperforms most 5★ for pure shield
  • Goal: 50,000+ HP

FAQ

Black Tassel or Staff of Homa?

Black Tassel for shield, Homa for hybrid. Homa provides ~19% less shield strength but adds meaningful burst damage. If your Zhongli is purely a shield bot, Black Tassel is better. If you want some damage, use Homa.

Is Zhongli still worth pulling in 5.7?

Yes, if you play Hu Tao, Xiao, Yoimiya, or any interruption-sensitive DPS. His universal 20% RES shred makes him valuable in nearly every team. The only teams that don't want him are Nilou Bloom and some Dendro reaction teams.
