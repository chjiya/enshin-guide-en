Why Zhongli Is Essential

Zhongli has the strongest shield in Genshin Impact. At high investment, his Jade Shield absorbs 20,000+ damage and has 100% uptime. It also shreds enemy elemental RES by 20% — a universal damage buff for your whole team.

Shield Bot Build

  • Artifact: 4-piece Tenacity of the Millelith — ATK buff on skill hit
  • Main Stats: HP% / HP% / HP% — stack as much HP as possible
  • Weapon: Black Tassel (3-star, HP% substat) or Staff of Homa
  • Goal: 50,000+ HP for an unbreakable shield

Best Teams

Zhongli fits in literally any team. He's especially valuable with:

  • Hu Tao — low HP playstyle needs a strong shield
  • Xiao — loses HP during burst, needs protection
  • Yoimiya — ranged attacker who hates getting interrupted
  • Any Hypercarry — the 20% RES shred is a free damage boost
