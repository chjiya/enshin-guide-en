Bennett Guide — The 6-Star Character

[Ad Placeholder]

Why Bennett Is Broken

Bennett's burst creates an Inspiration Field that heals and provides a massive ATK buff based on his base ATK. At C1 with a high-base-ATK sword, this buff can exceed 1,000 ATK — effectively doubling your DPS's damage. He also heals fast enough to keep anyone alive.

C6 Controversy

Bennett's C6 infuses melee attacks with Pyro inside his burst. This is great for Pyro DPS characters but ruins Physical and some Infusion-based teams (Ayaka, Eula). If you use these characters, don't activate his C6.

Build

  • Artifact: 4-piece Noblesse Oblige — team ATK buff on burst
  • Weapon: Aquila Favonia or Sapwood Blade (craftable)
  • Main Stats: ER% / HP% / Healing Bonus or HP%
