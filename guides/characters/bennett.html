Bennett Guide — The 6-Star (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Bennett's burst provides a 1000+ ATK buff at high investment — effectively doubling any ATK-scaling DPS. He also heals 5000+ per tick. He's been the most-used 4-star in Abyss since 1.0, with usage rates often exceeding limited 5-stars.

Community Consensus

KQM considers Bennett the best character in the game on a value-per-primogem basis. His only weakness is Circle Impact — you have to stay inside his burst radius. This makes him less optimal for mobile DPS like Neuvillette or Wanderer.

FAQ

Should I C6 my Bennett?

Only if you don't play Physical or non-Pyro infusion DPS. C6 gives 15% Pyro DMG and Pyro infusion inside his burst — great for Xiangling, ruins Eula/Ayaka/Keqing. In 2026, most meta teams are Pyro-friendly, so C6 is increasingly safe.
