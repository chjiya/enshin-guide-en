Neuvillette Guide — Hydro Sovereign

Why Neuvillette Is the Best DPS

Neuvillette's charged attack is a massive Hydro beam that pierces enemies. It scales purely off HP, can be aimed freely, and heals him while active. He's tanky, self-sufficient, and deals top-tier damage with minimal support.

How to Play

Use his skill and burst to generate Sourcewater Droplets. Absorb droplets to charge his special charged attack instantly (normally takes 4 seconds to charge). Fire the beam — hold it as long as possible. Repeat.

Best Team

  • Neuvillette — Main DPS
  • Furina — DMG% buff, Hydro resonance
  • Kazuha — VV shred + grouping
  • Zhongli / Baizhu — Shield for interruption resistance

Artifact & Weapon

  • Artifact: 4-piece Marechaussee Hunter — 36% CRIT Rate from HP changes
  • Weapon: Tome of the Eternal Flow or Prototype Amber (F2P!)
