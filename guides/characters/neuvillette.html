Neuvillette Guide — Hydro Sovereign (v5.7)

[Ad]

TL;DR

Neuvillette is the best solo DPS in Genshin. His charged attack is a massive Hydro beam that pierces enemies, scales purely off HP, and heals himself. At C0 with Prototype Amber (free craftable), he solo-clears Abyss Floor 12 — the only character who can do this consistently.

Community Consensus

SS-tier since 4.1. Community speedrun data shows Neuvillette teams have the fastest average Abyss clear times. His HP scaling makes him tanky (35K+ HP naturally) while dealing top-tier damage.

Optimal Team

Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha + Zhongli/Baizhu. Furina's DMG% buff stacks multiplicatively with Neuvillette's HP scaling.

FAQ

Neuvillette or Arlecchino?

Neuvillette for comfort, Arlecchino for speed. Neuvillette is tanky, self-healing, and has massive AoE. Arlecchino has higher single-target damage. Get both if you can — they cover different Abyss sides perfectly.
