Azhdaha Boss Guide

[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Azhdaha is a weekly boss found in Liyue. He's a massive Geo dragon that absorbs elements and changes attack patterns accordingly. A strong shield is mandatory — without one, his attacks deal damage over time that will kill your team.

Elemental Phases

Azhdaha absorbs 2 elements per fight: Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro. Each combination changes his attack patterns. Pyro phase includes exploding stomps; Cryo phase freezes the ground. The element he absorbs is random each week.

Best Teams

  • Must have: A shielder — Zhongli, Diona, or Noelle
  • Ranged DPS preferred — Azhdaha has large AoE attacks
  • Ganyu or Yoimiya work well for safe distance DPS

Tips

  • When he slams the ground, run to the edge of the arena
  • His tail slam has a huge hitbox — dodge sideways, not backwards
  • Save bursts for after he switches elements — that's your damage window
