Raiden Shogun Boss Guide

Overview

The Raiden Shogun weekly boss (Magatsu Mitake Narukami no Mikoto) is one of the hardest fights in Genshin. She has two phases: human form and the Baleful Shadowlord. The second phase includes an insta-kill attack if you don't manage the mechanic correctly.

Phase 2: Baleful Shadowlord

She summons a massive form. During this phase, she periodically summons an "Orb of Calamity" — DESTROY IT IMMEDIATELY. If you don't, she one-shots your entire team. Electro attacks deal bonus damage to the orb.

Best Teams

  • Bring Electro for the orb mechanic
  • Ranged characters help dodge her massive AoE slashes
  • Healer is essential — she deals unavoidable damage
  • Example: Yoimiya + Fischl + Zhongli + Bennett
