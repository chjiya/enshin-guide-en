Scaramouche Boss Guide

[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Scaramouche (Shouki no Kami) is the Sumeru archon quest final boss. The fight has a unique mechanic: you collect energy orbs with a pet (Neo Akasha Terminal) to break his shield in Phase 2.

Phase 1

Scaramouche in his humanoid mech form. Attack his feet to build up stagger. When staggered, climb onto the platforms and attack his core. Rinse and repeat.

Phase 2

His giant god form. You control the Neo Akasha Terminal — collect energy orbs on the ground to charge it, then fire to break his shield. Once the shield is down, he falls and you deal massive damage.

Tips

  • Dendro reactions are very effective
  • During Phase 2 orb collection, prioritize blue orbs (more energy)
  • Save bursts for his downed state after shield break
